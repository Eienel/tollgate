Tollgate Ledger

On-chain, read only, Pharos Atlantic

Read straight from the chain.
Verify a settlement. Count what a merchant earned.

This is the ledger, not the pitch. It does two things, both against live Pharos Atlantic in your browser: confirm that a Tollgate settlement really landed on-chain, and count the payments made to any merchant address. No backend, no wallet connection. See how Tollgate works.

Verify a settlement

Paste a settlement transaction hash. The ledger reads its receipt straight from the Atlantic RPC and confirms the ERC-20 transfer that paid the gate. Pre-filled with a real Tollgate payment from this network.

Press verify to read this settlement from the chain.
